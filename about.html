All About Us

Local people, local stories.

We are a small startup airline offering adventure flights, transport and sight-seeing, based in the scenic capital of New Zealand.

 

Who We Are

 
 
Olga McKenzie
Bruce McKenzie
 
 

Fly to the Limit is owned by a husband and wife team, Olga and Bruce McKenzie. Both of us were born in Southland and travelled extensively. We also both have commercial pilot licenses with 19 and 9 years respectively, flying for Middle eastern airlines, so our business is founded on passion and exeprience. Every plane is owner-operated, with safety and maintenance the highest priority. Our operators are all trained in sustainable tourism and share a love of the mountains and our peaceful scenery.
